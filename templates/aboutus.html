Our History

Malla Reddy University (MRU), established in 2020 in Hyderabad, Telangana, is a premier private university under the Malla Reddy Group of Institutions. Spread across a sprawling 100-acre campus, MRU offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, management, sciences, humanities, agriculture, and allied health sciences.

The university is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and has earned an NAAC A++ grade, reflecting its strong commitment to academic excellence and quality education. MRU fosters innovation and entrepreneurship through its state-of-the-art laboratories, research centers, and industry tie-ups with companies like Microsoft, Amazon, and TCS. With modern infrastructure, highly qualified faculty, and a strong placement record, MRU is emerging as one of the top destinations for higher education in South India.

Our Mission

Mallareddy University is committed to providing transformative education that empowers students to become leaders in their fields. We foster an environment of intellectual curiosity, innovation, and social responsibility.

Through our rigorous academic programs, cutting-edge research initiatives, and strong industry partnerships, we prepare our students to meet the challenges of a rapidly evolving global landscape.
